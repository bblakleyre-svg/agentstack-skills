## Purpose
Write compelling, accurate, MLS-compliant property descriptions that attract qualified buyers and position every home at its highest perceived value.

---

## When To Use
- New listing going live on MLS
- Price reduction requiring a reframed value narrative
- Listing has gone stale and needs fresh copy
- Seller requests a copy review

---

## Instructions

### Information to Gather First
- Bed/bath count, square footage, lot size, year built
- Recent renovations with years (kitchen 2022, roof 2021, etc.)
- Unique features: views, pool, garage, storage, outdoor space
- Neighborhood highlights: schools, walkability, commute access
- Seller's top 3 favorite things about the home
- Price tier: entry, mid-market, or luxury

### Description Structure
1. **Hook** — single most compelling feature (1 sentence)
2. **Home overview** — layout, condition, standout finishes (2–3 sentences)
3. **Key features** — top 3–5 specifics (flowing or bulleted)
4. **Location/lifestyle** — neighborhood context (1–2 sentences)
5. **Call to action** — urgency close (1 sentence)

---

## Examples

**Entry/Mid-Market:**
> Don't miss this move-in-ready [X bed / X bath] [home type] in the heart of [neighborhood]. Freshly updated with [renovation], the open-concept layout offers [feature] and [feature] throughout. Enjoy [outdoor space / garage / storage]. Minutes to [school / highway / amenity]. Schedule your showing today before it's gone.

**Luxury/Premium:**
> Sophistication meets comfort in this stunning [X bed / X bath] residence in [neighborhood]. Thoughtfully designed with [finish], [architectural detail], and [standout feature], every detail has been considered. The [primary suite / chef's kitchen / outdoor living] is a true showpiece. [Lifestyle sentence]. A rare opportunity in one of [city]'s most sought-after communities.

**Price Reduction Reframe:**
> Priced to move — and move fast. This [X bed / X bath] home in [neighborhood] represents exceptional value at $[price]. [Lead with best feature]. Same great home, better opportunity. Come see it before it's gone.

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Aspirational but honest — never oversell what isn't there
- Active voice, present tense — "the kitchen features" not "the kitchen is featured by"
- Specific over vague — "$50K renovation" beats "recently updated"
- Read aloud before submitting — catches awkward phrasing every time

## MLS Compliance
- No fair housing violations — no mention of religion, ethnicity, family status, nationality
- Replace "walking distance" with "minutes to" or "steps from"
- No unsubstantiated superlatives — "one of the area's finest" not "the best"
- Verify character limits with local MLS — default to 1,000 characters
