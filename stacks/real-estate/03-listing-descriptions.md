# Skill: Listing Descriptions
**Stack:** Real Estate Agent Stack
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Trigger:** When creating or updating a property listing.

---

## Purpose
Write compelling, accurate, and MLS-compliant property descriptions that attract qualified buyers and position the home at its highest perceived value.

---

## Instructions

### Information to Gather Before Writing
- Square footage, bed/bath count, lot size
- Year built, recent renovations (with years)
- Unique features: views, pool, garage, storage, outdoor space
- Neighborhood highlights: schools, walkability, commute access
- Seller's favorite things about the home
- Price positioning: entry, mid, or luxury market

### Description Structure
1. **Hook** (1 sentence) — lead with the single most compelling feature
2. **Home overview** (2–3 sentences) — layout, condition, standout finishes
3. **Key features** (3–5 bullet points or flowing sentences)
4. **Location/lifestyle** (1–2 sentences)
5. **Call to action** (1 sentence)

### Templates by Price Tier

**Entry/Mid Market:**
> Don't miss this move-in-ready [X bed / X bath] [home type] in the heart of [neighborhood]. Freshly updated with [renovation], the open-concept layout offers [feature] and [feature] throughout. Enjoy [outdoor space / garage / storage detail]. Minutes to [school / highway / amenity]. Schedule your showing today before it's gone.

**Luxury/Premium:**
> Sophistication meets comfort in this stunning [X bed / X bath] residence nestled in [neighborhood]. Thoughtfully designed with [high-end finish], [architectural detail], and [standout feature], every detail has been considered. The [primary suite / chef's kitchen / outdoor living area] is truly a showpiece. [Lifestyle sentence]. A rare opportunity in one of [city]'s most sought-after communities.

---

## MLS Compliance Rules
- No fair housing violations — never mention religion, ethnicity, family status
- No "walking distance" — use "minutes to" or "steps from"
- No subjective superlatives without support ("best" → "one of the area's most")
- Character limits vary by MLS — default to 1,000 characters, verify locally

---

## Notes
- Always read the description aloud before submitting — catches awkward phrasing
- Photos and description must align — don't describe features not shown
- Update description after price reductions to reframe the value story
