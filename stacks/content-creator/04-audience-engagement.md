## Purpose
Build a community, not just a following — turning passive viewers into engaged fans who share your content, buy your products, and stay through algorithm changes.

---

## When To Use
- Comments need to be responded to
- Community feels quiet or disengaged
- Launching something and need to activate the audience
- Direct messages need triage and responses

---

## Instructions

### Comment Response Cadence
- Respond to all comments within first 3 hours of posting (signals the algorithm)
- Prioritize: questions first, then thoughtful comments, then reactions
- Never ignore a comment that reveals a pain point — that's a content idea

### Comment Response Formula
1. Acknowledge what they said (show you actually read it)
2. Add one piece of new value — a tip, a resource, a follow-up thought
3. Ask a follow-up question to extend the conversation

### DM Triage
| Message Type | Response |
|--------------|----------|
| Question answerable in 1–2 sentences | Answer directly |
| Complex question | "Great question — I cover this in [link]" |
| Collaboration inquiry | "Thanks for reaching out! Here's how I handle collabs: [link or brief]" |
| Spam / solicitation | Ignore or block |
| Genuine fan / story | Respond personally — these become your loudest advocates |

### Community Activation Tactics
- Ask a question in every post — never just broadcast
- Pin a conversation-starting comment on every video within first hour
- Create a recurring community element (weekly Q&A, monthly challenge, etc.)
- Feature community members — spotlight comments, share wins, repost UGC

---

## Examples

**Comment Response (question):**
> Great question! The key thing most people miss is [insight]. If you want to go deeper on this, I actually broke it down step by step in [title/link]. What's your current setup?

**Comment Response (encouragement):**
> This made my day — thank you for sharing that. It's exactly why I make this content. Curious: what's the next thing you're working on with this?

**Community Question Post:**
> Quick question for you: what's the ONE thing holding you back from [goal]? Drop it below — I read every one, and this week's video might be your answer.

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Respond like a human, not a brand — use their name, be specific
- Give more than you take in every comment interaction
- Never make engagement feel like a performance — be genuinely curious
