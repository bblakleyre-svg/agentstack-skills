## Purpose
Build and maintain a content strategy that grows your audience, serves your community, and supports your monetization goals — without burning out.

---

## When To Use
- Starting or resetting your content direction
- Quarterly planning cycle
- Audience growth has stalled and direction is unclear
- Adding a new platform or content format

---

## Instructions

### Strategy Foundation (define once, revisit quarterly)
- **Primary platform:** Where does your best audience live?
- **Content pillars:** 3–4 recurring topics you own (e.g., productivity, mindset, tools, behind-the-scenes)
- **Audience avatar:** Who are you making this for? What do they want to be, do, or have?
- **Unique angle:** What's your specific perspective that no one else brings?
- **Monetization model:** Sponsorships, course, community, products, consulting?

### Publishing Cadence by Platform
| Platform | Sustainable Cadence |
|----------|-------------------|
| YouTube | 1–2x per week |
| Podcast | 1x per week |
| Newsletter | 1x per week |
| Instagram | 4–5x per week |
| TikTok | 1–3x per day (growth phase) |
| LinkedIn | 3–5x per week |

### Content Calendar Structure
- Week 1: Pillar content (long-form flagship)
- Week 2: Derivative content (clips, threads, repurposed)
- Week 3: Audience-driven (Q&A, comments, requests)
- Week 4: Behind-the-scenes / personal / story

---

## Examples

**Quarterly Strategy Review Prompt:**
> This quarter: What worked? (top 3 pieces by reach/engagement) What didn't? What did the audience keep asking for? What do I want to be known for in 90 days? What's the one format or platform I'll double down on?

**Content Pillar Definition:**
> Pillar: [Topic] | Target: [Audience segment] | Format: [Video/Post/Article] | Monetization link: [How this supports revenue]

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Strategy before tactics — don't add platforms until you've mastered one
- Batch creation beats daily scrambling — block creation days, not creation minutes
- Audience questions are your content calendar — read every comment
