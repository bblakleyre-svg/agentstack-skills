## Purpose
Attract, negotiate, and execute brand partnerships that pay well, stay authentic, and don't damage the audience trust you've built.

---

## When To Use
- Inbound brand inquiry received
- Proactively pitching a brand you want to work with
- Negotiating rates or deliverables
- Managing an active sponsorship

---

## Instructions

### Inbound Inquiry Process
1. Respond within 24 hours — express interest, ask for their brief
2. Review the brief: fit with audience, product quality, exclusivity requirements
3. Send your media kit and rate card
4. Negotiate: price, deliverables, usage rights, approval process
5. Get contract signed before creating any content
6. Deliver on deadline, send performance report within 7 days

### Rate Calculation Framework
- Base rate: $[X] per 1,000 followers / subscribers (varies by platform and niche)
- YouTube: $20–50 CPM on sponsorship read
- Instagram: $100–500 per 10K followers per post
- Newsletter: $30–100 per 1,000 subscribers per mention
- Negotiate: exclusivity, usage rights, and approval rounds all add cost

### Non-Negotiables Before Saying Yes
- Have you personally used or would use the product?
- Does the brand align with your audience's values?
- Are the approval requirements reasonable?
- Is the compensation fair for your time and reach?

### Deliverables Checklist
- Agreed content format and length
- Talking points approved by brand
- Disclosure language (required by FTC — always)
- Posting date and time
- Links/codes to include
- Performance metrics to report back

## Examples

**Inbound Response Email:**
> Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out — I'm familiar with [Brand] and I think there could be a great fit here. I'd love to see your brief. In the meantime, I've attached our media kit and rate card. Happy to jump on a quick call to align on goals.

**Proactive Pitch:**
> Hi [Name], I'm [Creator] — I create [content type] for [audience]. I've been using [Product] for [time] and it's genuinely changed how I [outcome]. I'd love to explore a partnership. Here's a quick overview: [media kit link]. Let me know if this sounds interesting.

**FTC Disclosure (always include):**
> This portion of the video is sponsored by [Brand]. [Honest description of what they do and why you recommend them.]

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Say no more than you say yes — scarcity protects your rates and your credibility
- Negotiate from value, not desperation — know your floor before any conversation
- Treat brand contacts like long-term relationships — they remember how you show up
