## Purpose
Build multiple revenue streams from your audience — moving from platform-dependent ad revenue to owned, sustainable income that compounds over time.

---

## When To Use
- Planning a new revenue stream
- Launching a product, course, or membership
- Evaluating a partnership or deal
- Annual revenue planning and diversification review

---

## Instructions

### Revenue Stream Hierarchy (build in this order)
1. **Sponsorships** — lowest friction, fastest to cash, least scalable
2. **Digital products** — courses, templates, guides — high margin, scales infinitely
3. **Membership/community** — recurring revenue, deepest audience relationship
4. **Consulting/coaching** — highest dollar per hour, limited by time
5. **Physical products** — highest complexity, but highest brand loyalty

### Launch Framework (for any product)
- **Week -2:** Tease on all platforms — problem-focused content
- **Week -1:** Build anticipation — behind-the-scenes, early interest list
- **Day 0:** Launch email + social + video
- **Days 1–3:** Social proof push — early testimonials, user results
- **Day 5:** Urgency — closing the cart or raising the price
- **Day 7:** Final push — last call email

### Pricing Psychology
- Anchor high, discount strategically — never start at your lowest price
- Odd prices convert better than round: $197 beats $200
- Bundles increase AOV — pair a core product with a complementary add-on
- Annual plans for memberships beat monthly — offer a 20% discount to drive uptake

## Examples

**Pre-Launch Tease Post:**
> I've been working on something for the last [time] that I genuinely wish existed when I was starting out. It's almost ready. If you're struggling with [problem], follow along this week. 👀

**Launch Email:**
> Subject: It's live — [Product Name]
> Hi [Name], [Product] is officially available. Here's what it is: [one sentence]. Here's who it's for: [specific person with specific problem]. Here's what's inside: [3 bullets]. Here's the link: [CTA]. Early bird pricing ends [date].

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Sell the outcome, not the content — "you'll be able to X" not "this course has 47 lessons"
- Urgency must be real — fake scarcity destroys trust permanently
- The best launch asset is proof — social proof, screenshots, results beat all copy
