## Purpose
Build a local following, drive reservations and walk-ins, and make your restaurant feel alive online — turning your social presence into a front-of-house extension that never closes.

---

## When To Use
- Daily social posting
- Specials or events need to be promoted
- A guest photos or tags the restaurant
- A promotion or campaign needs content

---

## Instructions

### Platform Focus for Restaurants
| Platform | Priority | Best Content |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Instagram | Primary | Food photography, Reels, stories |
| Google Profile | Primary | Photos, posts, review responses |
| Facebook | Secondary | Events, community, promotions |
| TikTok | Growth | Behind-the-scenes, chef content, viral moments |

### Content Pillars
1. **Food** — hero shots of dishes, specials, seasonal items
2. **People** — team, chef, regulars (with permission)
3. **Behind-the-scenes** — prep, plating, market runs, mise en place
4. **Community** — local events, partnerships, neighborhood love
5. **Promotion** — specials, events, reservations CTA

### Posting Cadence
- Instagram: 4–5x/week (2–3 posts, 2 Reels or Stories)
- Google: 1–2x/week
- Facebook: 3x/week
- TikTok: 3–5x/week during growth phase

### Food Photography Rules
- Natural light whenever possible
- Shoot immediately after plating — steam and color fade fast
- Clean plate, no smudges, garnish intentional
- Vertical format for Stories/Reels, square for feed

## Examples

**Specials Post:**
> Tonight only: [Dish name] 🌿
> [2-line description — sensory, specific]
> Available while they last — reserve your table now: [link]
> [Hashtags: #localrestaurant #[city]eats #[cuisine]]

**Behind-the-Scenes Caption:**
> 7am. Before the guests, before the rush — this is where tonight happens.
> [Tag chef if appropriate]
> Reservations for tonight: [link in bio]

**UGC Repost:**
> When a guest captures it better than we do 📸 Thank you @[handle] — hope to see you again soon!
> [Link to reservations]

**Event Promotion:**
> We're hosting [event] on [date] at [time]. [1-sentence what it is]. Seats are limited — reserve yours here: [link]

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Local and personal — people eat at restaurants they feel connected to
- Show the humans behind the food — personality drives loyalty more than photography
- Respond to every comment and tag within 2 hours — social is a two-way conversation

---

## Trigger Handoffs
- Guest tags a complaint publicly → hand off to **04-customer-feedback.md** immediately
- Event generates interest → route to **01-reservations.md** for booking
- Food photo goes viral → boost it, capture the email list opportunity
