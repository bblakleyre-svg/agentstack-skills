## Purpose
Handle reservation requests, confirmations, and modifications professionally — filling the floor efficiently, reducing no-shows, and making guests feel welcomed before they walk in the door.

---

## When To Use
- Guest calls, texts, or messages to make a reservation
- Reservation confirmation cycle is running
- Guest needs to modify or cancel a reservation
- Special occasion or dietary need is noted

---

## Instructions

### Reservation Information to Collect
- Guest name (first and last)
- Party size
- Date and time requested
- Phone number and/or email
- Occasion (birthday, anniversary, business — offer to note this)
- Dietary restrictions or allergies
- Seating preference (indoor, outdoor, booth, bar — if available)

### Confirmation Sequence
| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| At booking | Confirmation text or email |
| 24 hours before | Reminder with option to confirm or cancel |
| 2 hours before | Day-of reminder (for parties of 6+) |

### No-Show and Cancellation Protocol
- No-show: attempt to contact within 30 minutes
- Late cancellation (under 2 hours): note in system
- Repeat no-shows: require credit card to hold for future bookings
- Large party no-show → evaluate whether deposit policy is needed

---

## Examples

**Booking Confirmation Text:**
> Hi [Name], your reservation at [Restaurant] is confirmed for [date] at [time] for [X] guests. We're looking forward to seeing you! Reply to this message if you need to make any changes.

**24-Hour Reminder:**
> Hi [Name], just a reminder of your reservation at [Restaurant] tomorrow, [date] at [time] for [X] guests. We can't wait to see you! To cancel or modify, call us at [number] or reply here.

**Special Occasion Note to Staff:**
> Table [X] — [Name], party of [X] at [time]. Celebrating a [birthday/anniversary]. Please have a [candle/dessert/card] ready. Guest mentioned [dietary note if any].

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Warm from first contact — the reservation experience is the first course
- Specific and confirmatory — repeat date, time, and party size back every time
- Gracious about cancellations — how you handle changes is remembered

---

## Trigger Handoffs
- Large party or special event → hand off to manager or events coordinator
- Dietary/allergy noted → flag immediately for kitchen, note on reservation
- No-show → update table status, attempt contact, note for future policy
