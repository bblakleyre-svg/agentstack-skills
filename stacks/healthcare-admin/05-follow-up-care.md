## Purpose
Close the loop on every patient visit with follow-up that improves outcomes, increases adherence, and builds the kind of patient loyalty that drives referrals and retention.

---

## When To Use
- After any clinical visit (same-day or next-day follow-up)
- After a procedure or treatment
- When test results need to be communicated
- When a referral has been placed and needs tracking

---

## Instructions

### Post-Visit Follow-Up Sequence
| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Same day (after visit) | Summary of visit + instructions |
| 24–48 hours | Check-in: "How are you feeling?" |
| Per provider order | Lab results or test results delivery |
| 2 weeks | Follow-up appointment reminder if needed |
| 1 year | Annual wellness visit reminder |

### What a Follow-Up Message Should Include
- Brief summary of what was discussed or done
- Any action items for the patient (medication, exercises, diet changes)
- When to call if symptoms worsen
- Next appointment date if scheduled
- Provider or nurse contact for questions

### Test Results Protocol
- Normal results: deliver via patient portal or text with brief explanation
- Abnormal results: call the patient first, do not deliver via text only
- Critical results: contact provider immediately, then patient

---

## Examples

**Post-Visit Summary Message:**
> Hi [Name], thanks for coming in today. Here's a quick summary from your visit with [Provider]: [1–2 sentences on what was addressed]. Your next steps: [action items]. If you experience [specific symptoms], call us at [number] right away. Your follow-up appointment is scheduled for [date]. Take care!

**24-Hour Check-In:**
> Hi [Name], just checking in after your visit yesterday. How are you feeling? If you have any concerns or questions about your care plan, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here for you.

**Lab Results (normal):**
> Hi [Name], your [test name] results are in and everything looks normal. [Provider] has reviewed them. No action is needed — your next appointment is [date]. Have questions? Message us through the portal or call [number].

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Caring and clear — patients are often anxious post-visit
- Action-oriented — every message ends with "here's what to do next"
- Urgent when needed — don't soften critical information
