## Purpose
Create platform-appropriate social media content that builds brand awareness, drives traffic, and converts followers into customers — without sounding like an ad.

---

## When To Use
- Weekly content calendar needs to be filled
- New product launch requires a content push
- Promotion or sale needs social amplification
- UGC or customer content needs to be reposted with caption

---

## Instructions

### Content Mix (80/20 Rule)
- 80% value, entertainment, community, education
- 20% direct promotion and product push

### Platform Format Guide
| Platform | Format | Optimal Length | Best Content Type |
|----------|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| Instagram | Square/Reel | Caption: 125 chars above fold | Product lifestyle, UGC, Reels |
| TikTok | Vertical video | Hook in first 2 seconds | Tutorial, behind-the-scenes, trend |
| Facebook | Link + image | 40–80 words | Promotion, community, testimonials |
| Pinterest | Vertical image | Keyword-rich description | Product, tutorial, inspiration |
| X/Twitter | Text or image | Under 280 chars | Opinion, launch announcement, wit |

### Content Pillars
1. **Product** — features, benefits, use cases, new arrivals
2. **Education** — how-to, tips, industry knowledge
3. **Community** — UGC, reposts, customer spotlights
4. **Behind the scenes** — packing, team, sourcing, process
5. **Promotion** — sales, launches, limited offers

---

## Examples

**Product Launch (Instagram):**
> Meet [Product Name]. 👋
> [Benefit statement in one line.]
> We designed this for [target customer] who [pain point]. No more [problem]. Just [outcome].
> Available now — link in bio.
> [Relevant hashtags]

**UGC Repost Caption:**
> When our customers do the talking 🙌
> "[Customer quote or review]" — @[handle]
> This is exactly why we do what we do. Tag us in yours — we read every one.

**Educational Post:**
> 3 signs your [product category] isn't working for you anymore:
> 1. [Sign]
> 2. [Sign]
> 3. [Sign]
> If you checked even one — it might be time. [Link or CTA]

**Flash Sale (X/Twitter):**
> 24 hours. 30% off everything. No code needed.
> Sale ends [time]. [Link]

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Platform-native — Instagram can be warm, Twitter can be sharp, TikTok must be real
- Never sound like a press release — talk to people, not at them
- Short hooks, specific claims, clear CTA
- Emoji: purposeful not decorative — one or two max per post
