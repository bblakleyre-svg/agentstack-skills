## Purpose
Write product copy that converts browsers into buyers by leading with benefits, building desire, and removing purchase hesitation — optimized for both humans and search engines.

---

## When To Use
- New product being added to the store
- Existing product with low conversion rate
- Product listing being refreshed for a promotion
- SEO audit flags thin or duplicate content

---

## Instructions

### Information to Gather
- Product name, category, key materials/ingredients
- Primary benefit — what problem does it solve?
- Secondary features — what makes it stand out?
- Target customer — who buys this and why?
- Price positioning — budget, mid, or premium?
- Top customer questions or objections
- Primary keyword for SEO

### Description Structure
1. **Headline benefit** — one sentence, benefit-first (not feature-first)
2. **Short intro paragraph** — hook with the transformation or outcome (2–3 sentences)
3. **Key features as benefits** — 3–5 bullets, format: [Feature] — [why it matters to the buyer]
4. **Trust/credibility line** — materials, certifications, guarantee, or social proof stat
5. **CTA or urgency close** — what to do next

## Examples

**Mid-Market Product (skincare):**
> **Finally, a moisturizer that works overnight.**
>
> Wake up to visibly softer, more hydrated skin — without the greasy residue. Our [Product Name] uses pharmaceutical-grade hyaluronic acid to lock in moisture while you sleep, so your skin does its best work while you rest.
>
> - Hyaluronic Acid (3 weights) — penetrates surface, mid, and deep skin layers for all-day hydration
> - Ceramide Complex — repairs the moisture barrier and reduces flaking
> - Fragrance-free formula — safe for sensitive and reactive skin
> - Dermatologist-tested, non-comedogenic
>
> Over 4,000 customers have made this their nightly non-negotiable. Free returns if it's not right for you.
> [Add to Cart]

**Budget Product (household):**
> **The last [product type] you'll need to buy.**
> Durable, no-fuss, and built to handle daily use. [Product Name] delivers [key benefit] without the premium price tag. Works with [compatible uses]. [Material/durability claim]. Backed by our 1-year replacement guarantee.

## Tone Guidelines
- Benefits before features — always answer "so what?" for every feature listed
- Specific over vague — "4,200 five-star reviews" beats "customers love it"
- Match the voice to the brand — playful for lifestyle brands, precise for technical products
- Write for the skimmer first — headline and bullets must stand alone
