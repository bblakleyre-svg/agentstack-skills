## Purpose
Systematically request, respond to, and leverage customer reviews to build social proof, improve conversion rates, and protect brand reputation across all platforms.

---

## When To Use
- 7–14 days after product delivery (review request)
- Any time a new review is posted (positive or negative)
- When product pages need more social proof
- When a negative review requires public response

---

## Instructions

### Review Request Sequence
| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Day 7 post-delivery | Email review request |
| Day 14 post-delivery | Text follow-up (if no review left) |
| Day 30 | Final follow-up — personal tone |

### Priority Platforms
1. Google Business Profile — search visibility
2. Product page (on-site) — conversion rate
3. Trustpilot / Yelp (if applicable) — third-party trust
4. Amazon (if listed) — marketplace algorithm

### Response Protocol — Positive Reviews
- Respond within 24 hours
- Thank by name
- Reference the specific product or experience mentioned
- Reinforce one brand value
- Invite them back

### Response Protocol — Negative Reviews
- Respond within 2 hours
- Never be defensive
- Acknowledge the issue publicly
- Move resolution offline: "Please email us at [support email]"
- Follow up privately to resolve, then request update if warranted

---

## Examples

**Review Request Email (Day 7):**
> Subject: How's your [Product Name] treating you?
>
> Hi [Name],
> It's been about a week since your [Product Name] arrived — we hope you love it.
> If you have 60 seconds, your honest review would mean everything to us and helps other customers make confident decisions.
> [CTA: Leave a Review — takes 60 seconds]
> Thank you for being a customer. It genuinely matters.
> [Brand]

**Response to 5-Star Review:**
> Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled [specific thing they mentioned] is working so well for you — that's exactly what we designed it for. We can't wait to have you back. [Brand]

**Response to 1-Star Review:**
> Hi [Name], thank you for sharing your experience — this is not the standard we hold ourselves to and we want to make it right. Please email us directly at [support email] so we can resolve this for you personally. We'll be in touch within 24 hours. [Brand]

---

## Tone Guidelines
- Positive responses: warm, specific, brief — don't be robotic
- Negative responses: calm, accountable, solution-oriented — never defensive
- Never offer incentives for reviews — platform violations and trust damage
- Always respond publicly — future customers are reading, not just the reviewer

---

## Outputs
| Trigger | Output | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Review request trigger | Review ask email | Plain text |
| 5-star review received | Public response | Platform-native |
| 3-star review received | Response draft | Human review before posting |
| 1-star review received | Escalation brief | Human only — no auto-response |

---


## Trigger Handoffs
- Negative review with unresolved order issue → hand off to **08-returns-refunds.md**
- Customer leaves glowing review → request referral or UGC within 24 hours
- Review mentions competitor → do not engage publicly, note for product team
