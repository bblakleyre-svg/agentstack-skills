## Purpose
Write and sequence emails that build customer relationships, drive repeat purchases, and make every campaign feel personal — not like a mass blast.

---

## When To Use
- Weekly or bi-weekly newsletter needs to go out
- Product launch requires an email campaign
- Promotion or sale is running
- Post-purchase sequence needs to nurture new customers
- Win-back campaign needed for lapsed customers

---

## Instructions

### Campaign Types and Frequency
| Type | Frequency | Goal |
|------|-----------|------|
| Newsletter | Weekly or bi-weekly | Engagement + brand loyalty |
| Promotional | As needed (max 2x/week) | Revenue |
| Post-purchase | Triggered — day 1, 7, 30 | Retention + repeat purchase |
| Win-back | Triggered — 60/90 days inactive | Re-engagement |
| Product launch | 3-email sequence over launch week | Awareness + conversion |

### Email Structure (every email)
1. **Subject line** — specific, benefit-driven, 40 chars max
2. **Preview text** — extends the subject, adds curiosity
3. **Hero section** — one visual + one headline
4. **Body** — short, 3–5 sentences max per section
5. **Single CTA** — one button, one action
6. **Footer** — unsubscribe, address, social links

### Subject Line Formulas
- Curiosity: "We almost didn't make this..."
- Benefit: "Your skin will thank you tomorrow"
- Urgency: "Ends at midnight: 30% off"
- Social proof: "4,000 customers can't be wrong"
- Personalization: "[Name], this one's for you"

## Examples

**Welcome Email (Day 1 Post-Purchase):**
> Subject: You made a great call, [Name]
>
> Hi [Name],
> Welcome to [Brand]. Your order is being prepared and we'll send tracking as soon as it ships.
> While you wait — here's how to get the most out of your [product]: [tip or link]
> Questions? Hit reply. A real person reads every email.
> [Brand]

**Re-Engagement (60-Day Inactive):**
> Subject: We miss you (and we have something for you)
>
> Hi [Name],
> It's been a while. We've added [X new products / new collection / something we think you'll love] since your last visit.
> Here's 15% off to come back: WELCOMEBACK15
> [CTA: Shop Now]
> Valid for 7 days. No strings.

**Launch Email — Day 1:**
> Subject: It's here. [Product Name] is live.
>
> Hi [Name],
> [One-sentence description of what it does and why it matters.]
> We've been working on this for [time] and we're genuinely proud of it.
> [Key benefit 1. Key benefit 2. Key benefit 3.]
> [CTA: Shop Now — Early Access]
> First 48 hours: free shipping on all orders.

## Tone Guidelines
- Write like a person, not a brand — first person, direct, warm
- One email, one message — don't try to say everything
- Mobile-first — over 60% of email is read on phone, keep it short
- Test subject lines — even small changes move open rates significantly

## Trigger Handoffs
- Launch email sent → coordinate with **05-social-content.md** for same-day posts
- High click but no purchase → trigger **02-abandoned-cart.md** sequence
- Unsubscribe → remove from all sequences, log for list health tracking
